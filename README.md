## 👋 About

I'm an accountant at **Intuit, Inc.** and the owner of an independent accounting firm serving organizations in the Mid-Atlantic and South-Eastern Region. I hold various certifications including Intuit's **Advanced QuickBooks ProAdvisor** certification, Lean Six Sigma Green Belt, and ISC2 CC (Certified in Cybersecurity). My work centers on **accounting, accounting information systems and controls, as well as data analysis**.

My professional path bridges accounting, data analysis, and business strategy. I'm pursuing a multi-degree academic track at **Liberty University** culminating in a PhD in Organization and Management with a concentration in Entrepreneurship.

Before my career in accounting, I served as a Military Police Officer in the Virginia Army National Guard. In fact, I still serve my community as a volunteer firefighter with the [Hiltons VFD](https://hiltonsvfd.org/) and as the elected Club Secretary of the [Bristol Twin Cities Lions Club](https://bristollions.org/).
